Cold Coffee

Description

Frosty and satisfying cold coffee is a fantastic treat to enjoy on a warm day. This cold coffee recipe with step-by-by photos is a classic for preparing blended ice coffees that are perfectly sweet, creamy and frothy – just like from your favorite cafe!

cy cold coffee is simply the best beverage to enjoy on a hot summer day. Even though I tend to be more of a tea drinker, I do love a good cup of coffee occasionally – and this cafe-style blended coffee drink is my favorite!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Coffee
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Take the milk and add sugar and coffee adequately.
  2. Put some ice in the mixed milk
  3. put all these in the mixer
  4. Serve the coffee in a glass of your choice.
